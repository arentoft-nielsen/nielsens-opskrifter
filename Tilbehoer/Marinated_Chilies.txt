syltede chilier (Nielsens)

Ingredienser: 
200 g chilifrugter (evt. blandet)
3 hele hvidløg (i pillede/overskårne fed)
1 dl (balsamico) vineddike
1 dl eddike
1½ dl vand
4 spsk. soya-sauce
1 spsk. olie
2½ spsk. brun farin
3 tsk. (1 spsk.) salt
2 tsk. spidskommen
1½ tsk. hel koriander
4 tsk oregano
½ tsk. flydende Atamon
 
Tilberedning:
Smag på chilierne. Er de meget stærke, fjernes evt. frø+frøstreng, ellers befries de blot for
	stilke og skæres i skiver.
Eddike, vand, salt, krydderier, farin, olie og soya gives et opkog med chilier og hvidløgsfed.
Chilier og hvidløg tages fra og pakkes i glassene. Lagen gives et opkog og tilsættes Atamon
	efter kogning.
Væsken hældes over i glassene, som lukkes tæt.

Chilier, sursyltede

Ingredienser: 
100 g chili
1¾ dl vineddike
1¾ dl vand
1¾ spsk. sukker
¾ spsk. salt (soya-sauce?)
4-5 chalotteløg
1/4 tsk. Atamonpulver
eller 1/2 tsk. flydende Atamon
 
 
Tilberedning: 
Chilierne befries for stilke og prikkes med en gaffel, så væske bedre kan trænge ind.
Vineddike, vand, salt og sukker koger op med chilierne og de hele skrællede chalotteløg.
Væsken hældes fra og gives et opkog. Tilsættes Atamon. Chilier og løg lægges lagvis i glasset,
væske hældes over og glasset lukkes. Trækker ca. 4 uger.

MARINATED JALAPEÑO CHILIES
Homemade pickled chilies are easy to make and keep well for months in the refrigerator,
serve on an hors d'oeuvre tray, use to accompany foods, or cut into strips for tucking
into tacos. A jar of chilies also makes a great gift for food-loving friends. 

1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 pounds fresh jalapeno chili peppers 
1 large onion, thinly sliced  
2 large carrots, peeled and thinly sliced
1 head garlic, cut in half vertically and loose outer peel removed 
3 cups distilled white vinegar
1-cup water 
3 rounded tablespoons dried oregano, crumbled
¼ cup firmly packed dark brown sugar 
salt and freshly ground pepper  

In a large saucepan over medium heat, warm the oil. Add the chilies, onion, garlic and
carrot and sauté until tender-crisp, about 1 minute. Add the vinegar, water, oregano,
brown sugar and salt and pepper to taste and bring to a boil. Remove from the heat and
cover an let cool completely before serving. 

To store, pour into glass jars and cover tightly. The pickled chilies can be refrigerated
for up to 3 months. Makes about 8 cups

PICKLED CHILIES

INGREDIENTS 
 
15 chile güero or yellow wax chilies
4 small limes
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup water
olive oil 
 
PREPARATION 

Wash and dry the chile güero (5-6 cm long, in American supermarkets available as "yellow
wax chilies"). In a cup mix lime juice, soy sauce and water.

Heat olive oil in a small pan. Add the chilies and fry them until they are lightly brown
on all sides. Pour in the lime-soy-water-mixture and simmer for about 5 minutes.

Store the chilies in a small bowl in the fridge.
